Biography

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Heung-Yeung Shum. Currently, I am a Researcher at VAST. Prior to this, I spent nearly four years collaborating with Dr. Yang Liu, Dr. Hao Pan, Dr. Peng-Shuai Wang and Dr. Xin Tong at Microsoft Research Asia. I received my B.S. Degree in Physics from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University in 2020.

My research interest includes computer graphics and 3D computer vision.

I strive for the realization of the Chinese Dream of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation.

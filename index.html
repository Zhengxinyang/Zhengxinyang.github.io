Biography

I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate of the Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University, supervised by Dr. Heung-Yeung Shum. Currently, I am fortunate to work with Dr. Yang Liu, Dr. Hao Pan, Dr. Peng-Shuai Wang and Dr. Xin Tong at Microsoft Research Asia. Before that, I received my B.S. Degree in Physics from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University in 2020.

My research interest includes computer graphics and 3D computer vision.
